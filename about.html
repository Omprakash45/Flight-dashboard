Welcome to the Enhanced Flight Dashboard!
This dashboard combines the power of SQL, Python, Plotly, and Streamlit to offer a seamless experience for exploring and analyzing flight data. Whether you're a frequent traveler, a data enthusiast, or someone planning their next trip, this tool is designed with your needs in mind.

Key Features:

  • Check Flights: Quickly search flights between your preferred cities with details such as flight name, departure time, arrival time, duration, and price.
  • Flights Analytics: Discover insights with:
    • Interactive visualizations for price distribution.
    • Analyze flight frequency per airline.
    • Check average flight durations.
    • Understand peak departure times.
    • Explore price trends by departure time.

How to Use:

  1. Navigate to the desired section from the sidebar menu (e.g., "Check Flights" or "Flights Analytics").
  2. Select your source and destination cities from the dropdown menus.
  3. Click "Search" to view detailed flight information or explore interactive analytics.
  4. Enjoy intuitive charts powered by Plotly to make informed travel decisions.
